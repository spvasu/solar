About Us   SolarPV's Story

How We Began 

SolarPV began in 2020 when Kristofor Anderson and Donald Marovich met in a college class. They quickly realized that together, they could bring cutting edge solar technology to the wold, and beyond! Kris and Donald quickly formed a team and began work on the company, employing great legends such as Daniel Neal for consulting. This attracted the attention of many shareholders who took vestment in the company as SolarPV continued to make advances towards solar technology.

Where We Are Today

Today, SolarPV leads the entire world in the solar industry, providing installations in any location. Even Obama's latest satellite internet company is using our panels to power their computers and communications! The future looks bright with SolarPV leading the way!
